What are the Available membership options on Voicescity?

There's a broad selection of tiered memberships you can choose from Voicescity:


Membership Annual Price Job Invitations Customer Support
Basic Free 10 Email
Silver $199 30 Live Chat
Gold $299 Unlimited Live Chat & Phone Support

The main differences among these memberships are:

  1. INVITATIONS: The number of project invitations you receive. You will thus increase your chances of getting booked by a client because you’ll be able to submit more project proposals.
  2. SEARCH RESULTS: The number of times you appear in searches performed by clients. This will increase your visibility considerably as well as the chances of your profile card being clicked on by a client on the search page.
  3. COMPETITIVENESS: The number of voice actors you’ll be competing against. The higher the tier of your membership, the sooner you get invited to projects and the smaller the group of actors that get invited in each batch of email invitations.
  4. SUPPORT: The level of customer support you receive. The higher your membership tier, the sooner we’ll be able to address your queries and attend to your requests.

How to select the most suitable membership for you:

All voice actors start with a free, Basic membership. However, when you're ready to formalize your voice-over career and audition for more clients, you can choose to upgrade to one of our paid tier memberships.

The higher your paid membership, the more projects you'll be invited to. The same applies to the number of times you'll appear in searches performed by clients

Our Silver membership is our mid-level tier, provide you with space where you can upload and showcase samples, as well as reveal your work experience. As a Silver member, you'll receive limited invitations and can apply for limited matching jobs. The cost of the Silver membership is $199 per year.

Gold is our exclusive, top-level tier aimed at highly successful professionals. They're given preferred access to all projects matching their profile. There is a substantial waiting list and the cost of the gold membership is $299 per year.
